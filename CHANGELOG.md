## [0.1.0] - 2026-01-06

### Added
- Initial release with 12 read-only tools
- Portfolio and positions tracking
- Stock quotes, fundamentals, and historicals
- News, earnings, and analyst ratings
- Dividend history
- Options positions (read-only)
- Symbol search
- TOTP-based 2FA support
